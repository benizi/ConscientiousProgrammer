OpenHack Pittsburgh: Working on a Private Project

Feb 24th, 2014 | Comments

Last month at OpenHack Pittsburgh, I had a rather social time.

This month, I kept entirely to myself. I didn’t even speak up to say what I was working on. I was in a somber mood, and didn’t really want to talk. I worked on a private project that I will share when I am ready.

It did feel consoling to be around other people, even while keeping to myself. Sometimes I just need to know that I am not alone.
